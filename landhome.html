ABOUT US

A Legacy of Precision and Innovation

Founded in 1853 by Charles-Félicien Tissot and his son Charles-Émile Tissot, Tissot is a renowned watch brand from Le Locle, Switzerland. They transformed their home into a small factory, marking the beginning of Tissot's journey. In 1858, Charles-Émile expanded the brand to Russia, gaining popularity across the empire, including the Tsar's court. By 1885, Tissot's presence in Russia was solidified when Charles Tissot moved to Moscow to manage their branch. Now part of the Swatch Group, Tissot continues to embody Swiss watchmaking excellence, symbolizing precision, innovation, and timeless elegance. Tissot is not affiliated with Mathey-Tissot, another Swiss watchmaking company.
